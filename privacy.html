Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

Introduction

At Chaney Insurance, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you choose to provide it, such as through our contact forms. Information submitted via forms is handled securely and used only for the purpose intended (for example, responding to your inquiry).

Analytics

We use Simple Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Simple Analytics is a privacy-friendly analytics tool that does not track visitors across websites, does not collect personal data, and does not use cookies.

This allows us to measure traffic and improve our website while respecting your privacy.

Data Security

Any data you submit through forms is transmitted securely. We take reasonable measures to ensure your information is protected against unauthorized access or disclosure.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data you have shared with us. To exercise these rights, please contact us at admin@chaney-insurance.com.
